Welcome to Monster Card Battle!

Each game starts off with the player
starting with five cards in hand. The opponent
has one card on the playing field. Each
player must only play one card at a time.
The objective is to play a card with an Attack
Power higher than the opponents Defense Power.
If the attack is successful, the opponents card
will be defeated and the difference between the
Attack and Defense will be deducted from that players HP.
First player to reach 0 loses.
